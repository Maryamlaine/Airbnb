Naive Bayes Model





Applied a structure to our DataFrame to avoid dtype conflicts.





Created bins for review scores and labelled based on ranges of scores that we would later relate to positive or negative comments.





Removed undesireable punctuation, created function to identify english comments, and resized our data selection to english only.





After creating our tokens and features, we imported NaiveBayes and fit our now prepared dataframe to the model.





After training our model, results showed that we were able to predict review scores from English comments with 99.8% accuracy.
